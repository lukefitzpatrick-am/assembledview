# Short-form video specs and evidence (verified July 2026)

## Specs
- TikTok in-feed: 9:16 vertical only; ad text above video 80 chars rec (100 max); display name 40. Spark ads boost organic posts (keep the creator's caption). CTA from TikTok's predefined set.
- YouTube skippable in-stream: skip at 5 seconds; any length, 15-30s typical for performance.
- YouTube bumper: 6 seconds, unskippable. One message, brand throughout.
- YouTube Shorts / Meta Reels: 9:16, under 60s; safe zones roughly top 14% and bottom 20-35% free of text/logo.
- Captions/subtitles on everything: majority sound-off viewing on feeds; TikTok skews sound-on but caption anyway.

## Evidence
- Google ABCD framework (Attract, Brand, Connect, Direct): validated by Google/Ipsos and Nielsen studies across thousands of ads - following ABCD correlates with significant lift in short-term sales likelihood and brand metrics (exact uplift figures vary by study; below 90% confidence on any single number).
- Nelson-Field / Amplified: ~1.5s of attention can encode when distinctive assets appear early; a fully watched 6s beats partial viewing of a 20s (dentsu); 100% screen coverage ~2x sales impact vs 50%.
- TikTok's own guidance: native-feeling content outperforms polished ads; first 2 seconds determine watch; text overlay essential.
- Hook diagnostics (performance creative consensus): 3s view rate = hook quality; 50%/100% hold = narrative quality; CTR/CVR after good hold = offer problem.
- Wear-out: short-form fatigues in 2-4 weeks at scale; refresh hooks/first frames first (shared with assembled-meta-copy evidence).
