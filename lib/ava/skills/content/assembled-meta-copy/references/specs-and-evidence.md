# Meta specs and evidence base (verified July 2026)

## Specs
- Primary text: no meaningful hard cap; ~125 chars display before "See more" in Feed - the hook must land inside that. Headline field has no reliable hard cap (platform accepts ~255); keep 27-30 chars to avoid mobile clipping. Description ~30 chars shown, not on Stories/Reels.
- Flexible ads (replaced Dynamic Creative, DCO sunset 2024-25): up to 10 images/videos, 5 primary texts, 5 headlines, 5 descriptions, mixed per user. (One source reports the standalone flexible toggle folded into Advantage+ settings March 2026 - below 90% confidence, verify in Ads Manager.)
- Advantage+ creative: text generation makes up to 5 AI variants of copy; enhancements individually toggleable. Turn text-modifying enhancements off for regulated clients.
- Safe zones (9:16): keep text/logo out of ~top 14% and bottom 20-35%. FB/IG Stories+Reels unified to a single 9:16 safe zone in early 2026 (date below 90% confidence).
- Text-heavy images: the 20% rule is gone but text-heavy statics still underperform. Overlay 5-7 words max.

## Evidence and operating patterns
- Hook rule: first 3 seconds of video / first ~125 chars of text decide performance. Meta's recommended hook types: value promise, statement of intent, question/invitation.
- Andromeda (Meta's late-2025 ranking overhaul): creative diversity is the de facto targeting mechanism. Near-duplicates get limited delivery. Consensus: 1-3 ad sets, 10-20 genuinely distinct creatives each (ranges vary by source, ~85% confidence).
- Fatigue: top performers decay in 2-4 weeks post-Andromeda; frequency above 3 is the warning; refresh hooks/first frames before rebuilding concepts.
- Performance 5 (Meta's official baseline): simplified structure, broad targeting, creative diversification, Conversions API data quality, experiment-based measurement.
- Portfolio thinking (Foxwell/Hott synthesis): run native/UGC, founder story, polished brand, objection-handling and persona-specific ads simultaneously. "Ugly ads" are a corrective, not a rule. Diversify hook x format x messenger x message.
- Marketing brain overlay: Meta is a low-attention-elasticity environment - branding must land in the first 1.5s (Nelson-Field 2025), 100% screen coverage formats (Reels/Stories) beat feed statics for encoding, and passive-attention frequency does some work when reach is cheap.

## Hook frameworks
Problem call-out ("Still towing a van that sways?"), stat/social proof ("12,000 Aussie families switched"), contrarian ("Everything you've heard about off-grid is wrong"), curiosity gap, before/after, founder-on-phone story, objection pre-empt ("Yes, your SUV can tow this"), straight offer.
