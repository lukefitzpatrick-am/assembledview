# Example - 15s TikTok/Shorts script (one of three concepts)

Fictional client: Coastline Caravans. Objective: activation (enquiries), format: TikTok in-feed + Shorts cutdown. Concept: "Tow test" (objection pre-empt, UGC style).

## Hook variants (first 1-2s, brand decal visible from frame one)
1. "This family SUV just towed 2.4 tonnes." (visual: van hitched, odometer close-up)
2. "Everyone said we'd need a LandCruiser. Watch." (visual: sedan-adjacent SUV reversing to hitch)
3. "The tow test caravan salesmen don't show you." (visual: weighbridge ticket held to camera)

## Script (15s, two-column)
0.0-1.5 | Hitched van pulls away, Coastline decal centre frame | VO: "This family SUV just towed 2.4 tonnes." | Overlay: "Your SUV can tow this"
1.5-5.0 | Cut: weighbridge ticket, then dash view on highway | VO: "We weighed it, checked the specs, and matched the van to the car - before showing a single model." | Overlay: "Weighed. Matched. Legal."
5.0-10.0 | Walkthrough: kids in the van, storage opens | VO: "Sixty-plus vans in stock. They check your car first, then show you what actually works." | Overlay: "60+ in stock"
10.0-15.0 | Presenter to camera at yard, logo lockup | VO: "Book a free walkthrough at Coastline. Ask them anything." | Overlay + CTA: "Book a free walkthrough"

Caption: Your SUV probably tows more than you think. We check first. #caravan #towing
CTA button: Learn more.

## Editor notes
- Brand decal in frame from 0.0; logo lockup again at CTA (ABCD: brand early and late).
- Captions burned in, safe zones respected (nothing in top 14% / bottom 30%).
- Cutdowns: 6s bumper = hook 1 + overlay + lockup only. 30s in-stream = extend walkthrough beat; brand and hook stay ahead of the 5s skip.
- Ship alongside concepts 2 (founder yard tour) and 3 (owner testimonial) - never one style alone.
